About Me

Hi, I'm Alina Lee, a photographer and film maker based in Los Angeles, California. I specialize in capturing beautiful moments and stories through the lens of a camera. With a passion for creativity and a keen eye for detail, I strive to create visually stunning and emotionally resonant images that tell a story through the lens of a camera.

My work is a reflection of my love for photography and my dedication to capturing the essence of life through the lens of a camera.
